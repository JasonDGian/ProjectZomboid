[h1]Neco Arc Bludgeon - The Cataclysmic Zombie Crusher[/h1]

[h2]About This Mod:[/h2]
Introducing the [b]Neco Arc Bludgeon[/b], a [i]purrfect[/i] blend of adorable chaos and blunt-force trauma. Who knew a cute figure could be your ultimate weapon for survival? In the zombie apocalypse, this is your go-to for crushing the undead and adding a little [i]feline flair[/i] to your arsenal.

[h2]Features:[/h2]
[list] [*][b]Blunt Force Impact:[/b] Swing your Neco Arc figure like the ultimate bludgeoning weapon. With an [i]incredibly low minimum range[/i], you don’t need to aim—just [i]bap[/i] the zombies up close, and they’re done for! 
[*][b]Unique Sound Effects:[/b] Every strike comes with a ridiculous, yet oddly satisfying sound. Expect [i]“Meow! Thud!”[/i] followed by an [i]adorable “Purr!”[/i] as you turn zombies into mush. It’s the ultimate insult to the undead. 
[*][b]Neco Arc's Smug Face:[/b] Don’t just defeat your enemies—do it with style. Neco Arc’s [i]smug[/i] expression will haunt your foes as they wonder if being hit by a figure this cute was truly their worst fate. [/list]

[h2]Why You Need This:[/h2]
In the bleak world of zombie-slaying, sometimes you just need a little fun. The Neco Arc Bludgeon is more than a weapon—it’s a [i]mood[/i]. Swing it with pride, hear the bizarre sounds, and revel in the chaos. No zombie stands a chance when faced with the sheer [i]adorableness[/i] of your weapon.

[b]Warning:[/b] Zombies [i]really[/i] don’t like being hit by something so cute. But you’ll love it.
