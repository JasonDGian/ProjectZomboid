[h3]ğŸ“ Right click on the materials you wish to bind.[/h3]
Select, for example, "Bind planks x5" from the right-click context menu. You can perform this action from your inventory or on the ground.

[h3]ğŸ“ Look for the 'Organize' category.[/h3]
In both Build 41 and Build 42 you can access the crafting menu and search for the [b]Organize[/b] category to craft the bound stacks.
